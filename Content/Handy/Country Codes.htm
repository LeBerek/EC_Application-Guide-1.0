Country Codes

Codes below function for the international exchange of rail data between UIC (fr.)Union Internationale des Chemins de fer, (en.) International Union of Railways and OSJD (EN) Organization for Cooperation of Railways, (RU) Организация Сотрудничества Железных Дорог – ОСЖД members. These codes:

  • Are used as the 3rd and 4th digit of the EVN The unique number consists of 12-digits which identify technical characteristics of the railway vehicle. EVNs are managed in the European Vehicle Register (EVR). of wagons of the 1435 mm rail system, and inform about the country of the first registration of the wagon.
  • Can be found as a component of the RICS Railway Interchange Coding System number, assigned to some national infrastructure managers or to a national railway.
  • Describe respectively the ownership of broad-gauge wagons from the 1520 mm rail system.
